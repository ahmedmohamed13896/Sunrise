A Grand Opening Celebration with a Professional Networking Luncheon

Enjoy a Positive, Friendly Celebration and Introduction to Sunrise Detox. As professionals in substance abuse treatment, every day we help and try to help as many as we can. How about a day of celebration and community with your peers?

Come enjoy a catered Lunch, with refreshments, some essential guests and guest speakers, and your chance to win raffle prizes (including an Apple iPad Pro!). This page will be updated with more information about our special guests, the raffle prizes and time, and the delicious catered lunch.

Everyone Will Be There!

This is an event not-to-miss, with special guests and guest speakers, including the Worcester County District Attorney Joseph D. Early, Jr. (established the Worcester County Opioid Task Force, and the Memorial Wall project), and Senator Michael O. Moore (member of several committees including the Special Senate Committee on Opioid Addiction Prevention, Treatment and Recovery Options) and more to be announced! Hopefully, YOU will also be there! 

For your convenience, Sunrise Detox is providing professional Valet Service for this event.

An Incredible Professional networking Opportunity

Guests at previous Sunrise Detox, Open House events, have said that this is one of the best, most productive professional networking activities available to marketers, treatment providers, referring clinicians and practitioners, and involved community leaders in the industry.

We try to make this the “Meet and Greet of the Year” for treatment industry professionals, and we would love to see you there.
