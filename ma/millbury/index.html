OPENING EARLY NOVEMBER 2018:
A Free-Standing Luxury Medical Detox Center in Millbury

Our new 18,000 square foot substance abuse treatment center in Millbury, Massachusetts is a free-standing medical detox center for the treatment of alcohol and drug addiction disorders. Sunrise Detox delivers the essential first step on the road to recovery, when it is needed, in the most comfortable ways available.

We know that a comfortable detox is the best start of a successful recovery, so Sunrise Detox Millbury is designed to provide safe, professional detox in comfortable luxury, assisted by the best customer service available, and with care delivered by certified, experienced professionals.

At Sunrise Detox there are no hospital gowns, you have freedom to roam the premises, and you are treated with dignity and respect, receiving firm, compassionate encouragement from everyone.

Sunrise partners with all members of the treatment industry in the Continuum of Care. We accept most private insurance and offer attractive self-pay options. Come visit us and see for yourself why we have earned our strong reputation for working hard to help ensure that those in need of help get the treatment they need, when they are willing to accept it. Let us show you that we are unequaled in our commitment to supporting appropriate pre-existing patient treatment plans.
