Insurance and Payment Options for Benzo Detox

If you have any major insurance carrier, your treatment is likely covered.

If you'll self-pay, you can use cash, check, or credit card. The only thing we don't accept is Medicare or Medicaid because we're a private facility and don't receive any government funding.

An addiction advisor will check your insurance before you arrive, so there are no surprises.
